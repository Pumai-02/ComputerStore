About TechNova

Our Story

Technology, Curated for Real Builders

TechNova started as a two-person repair shop and grew into a destination for gamers, creators, and businesses who want genuine hardware, honest pricing, and advice from people who actually build and benchmark every product we sell.

Today we carry laptops, desktops, components, and accessories from eight leading brands, backed by official warranties and a support team that answers real questions — not scripts.
